🎧 Playlist: Summer

Summer is my least favorite season — humid, stormy, military drills under the sun.
But summer knows how to market itself: the longest break, the symbolic end of a chapter, and tag for youth.
It’s always in summer that I travel with friends and people I’ve had feelings for.
The heat turns into restless heartbeat, and the downpour becomes the romance of sharing an umbrella with someone.
